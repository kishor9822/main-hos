Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

Cannot find your answer? Book a free call and we will walk you through it.

How quickly can we see results from paid media?
Early signal usually appears within the first two to four weeks as campaigns exit the learning phase. Meaningful, stable results that you can scale typically land in the first sixty to ninety days, once we have enough conversion data to optimize toward qualified pipeline.
Which platforms do you manage?
Our core focus is Google Ads and Meta Ads, the two platforms that drive the most predictable performance for most brands. We also run LinkedIn and other paid channels where they fit your audience and goals.
How do you prevent wasted ad spend?
Every campaign starts from a strategy brief, with tracking and audience architecture set up before launch. We optimize toward qualified leads and revenue rather than clicks, pause what does not work quickly, and reallocate budget toward what does.
Do you handle the ad creative and copywriting?
We write the ad copy, the hooks and a clear creative brief for every campaign, so your designer knows exactly what to produce. The design and video assets themselves come from your side, and we test and optimise whatever you supply.
What is your approach to account management?
You work with a dedicated performance strategist, not a rotating support queue. You get weekly performance reporting focused on revenue and ROAS, plus a clear view of what we are testing and why.
How do you handle attribution in a post-iOS world?
We combine server-side tracking, GA4, and platform conversion APIs with CRM feedback, so we can connect ad spend to qualified pipeline and revenue even where browser-level signal has degraded.
Do you work with brands based in the UAE?
Yes. We are built for the UAE market: campaigns in English, Arabic and other native languages, billed and benchmarked in AED, with local audience data across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and the wider GCC.
