Common Questions

Straight answers before you commit.

The questions every smart client asks us first.

Is ad spend included in the monthly fee?
No, and that separation protects you. Our fee covers strategy, build, optimisation and reporting. Your advertising budget is paid by you directly to Google, Meta, LinkedIn or TikTok, so you always see exactly where every dirham of media goes.
How fast will we see leads?
Campaigns go live within 48 hours of access. First leads typically arrive in the first days, while stable, scalable results usually take 60 to 90 days as the platforms gather conversion data. We never promise specific numbers before your data exists.
Am I locked into a contract?
No. Every plan runs month to month. We keep clients with results, not with paperwork.
What do you need from us?
Access to your ad accounts and website, a clear offer, your design and video assets (we brief them, your team produces them), and a commitment to respond to leads quickly. Fast follow-up, ideally inside 15 minutes on WhatsApp, is the single biggest driver of results in this market.
Which industries do you focus on?
Our deepest focus is Dubai real estate and property finance lead generation, and we also run campaigns for healthcare, business setup, entertainment and podcast brands across the UAE.
