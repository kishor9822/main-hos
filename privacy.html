Privacy, in plain language.

Effective date: 3 July 2026. Questions about this page: hello@thehouseofscale.com.

Who we are

The House of Scale ("we", "us") is a performance marketing company serving clients in Dubai and across the UAE. This policy explains what personal data we collect through this website and how we use it.

Data we collect

  • Contact details you submit through our forms: name, email address, phone number and your message.
  • Messages you send us on WhatsApp or by email.
  • Basic usage data collected by analytics and advertising tools, such as pages visited, device type and approximate location.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide our services.
  • To operate, measure and improve this website and our marketing.
  • To send you relevant updates only where you have agreed to receive them.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with service providers who help us operate, such as website hosting, analytics and advertising platforms including Google and Meta, and only as needed to provide our services or meet legal obligations.

Cookies

This site may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and advertising measurement. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Retention and your rights

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us, and we will act in line with applicable data protection laws.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the latest version.
